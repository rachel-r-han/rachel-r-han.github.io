### Research Method

This project is based on multi-site field visits conducted between 2023–2026 across Shaanxi, Henan, Anhui, Jiangsu, Sichuan, Hubei, and Shanxi.

Methods include:
- On-site observation and repeated visits
- Informal visitor conversations
- Comparative spatial analysis
- Participation in restoration practice (60+ hours)
- Archival reading and local gazetteers
